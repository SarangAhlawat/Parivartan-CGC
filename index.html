About Us

Parivartan Fest at CGC Landran is a vibrant annual event that unites students, faculty, and the community. Held on the scenic campus of Chandigarh Group of Colleges, the fest offers a mix of tech challenges, workshops, and engaging seminars. Enjoy a range of cultural events, including dance, music, and art, showcasing student talents. This year's theme celebrates new ideas and positive change, making Parivartan a wonderful time to come together and create lasting memories.
