About Us

Parivartan Fest at CGC Landran is a vibrant annual event that unites students, faculty, and the community in a celebration of innovation, creativity, and unity. Held on the scenic campus of Chandigarh Group of Colleges, the fest offers a dynamic mix of tech challenges, workshops, and cultural events like dance, music, and art, showcasing diverse student talents. Its theme, A Crossroad of Cultures, represents a fusion of diverse inspirations, creating harmony and happiness while celebrating human expression and fostering positive change. Parivartan promises a mesmerizing experience, blending the mystical charm of the universe with the vibrancy of cultural diversity, inviting all to escape the ordinary and embark on an unforgettable adventure.
